Our Mission

UTHM Re-Trade is a platform created specifically for university students to buy, sell, and exchange academic items easily. Our goal is to help reduce material waste and make academic tools more affordable by providing a safe trading space restricted purely to UTHM students.

Platform Guidelines & Safety

  • Meet on Campus: Always choose public areas inside UTHM (like the library or student center) to exchange items safely.
  • Inspect Items First: Check books or electronics thoroughly before handing over money.
  • Keep it Academic: Only list education-related items like textbooks, official gear, and technical tools.
